How it works

Intercepts before it leaves the browser

No network proxies, no VPNs, no infrastructure changes. A lightweight Chrome and Edge extension that works instantly.

01

Employee types a prompt

Your staff opens ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, or Claude and starts typing their prompt as usual.

02

AI DLP Pro scans in real time

The extension scans every keystroke for PHI, PII, financial data, and secrets — before the prompt is sent.

03

Block, warn, or redact

Sensitive data is blocked, flagged for review, or automatically redacted based on your policy settings.

04

Every event is logged

Your compliance team gets a full audit trail of every AI interaction across your entire organization.

Features

Built for regulated industries

Healthcare, insurance, and legal organizations face unique compliance requirements. AI DLP Pro is built for all three.

🚫

Real-time blocking

Intercepts prompts at the keyboard before they reach the AI service. Zero network latency. Works entirely in the browser.

📄

File upload scanning

Scans Word, Excel, PDF, and CSV attachments before they're uploaded to any AI tool. Catches sensitive data in documents too.

🔒

PHI and PII detection

Detects SSNs, MRNs, ICD-10 codes, CPT codes, CDT dental codes, credit cards, API keys, and more — with context-aware classification.

📊

Centralized audit trail

Every block, warning, and override is logged with timestamp, user, tool, and finding details. Ready for HIPAA audits and SOC 2 reviews.

⚙

Per-tenant policy control

Set different policies per organization. Block PHI, warn on PII, allow financial data — fully configurable per client.

👥

Per-seat licensing

License per user per month. Seat limits enforced automatically. Add or remove seats instantly from the admin portal.
